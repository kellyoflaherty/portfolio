"Curse of Curves" 3d Animation

Short 3d animaiton created for a client with the intention of being showcased alongside their work during a fashion show/exhibition. The video's concept follows the idea behind the client's fashion collection; redesigning costumes for "pit girls" in racing. The colours used are also based upon the clients collection and inspiration.
I modelled this bike entirely within Maya with the exception of the engine which was sourced online. It was then composited with the addition of motion graphics in After Effects.

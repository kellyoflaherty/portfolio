Spook Screen Logo Animation

I created a short animation whilst working with DeepRed Productions in preparation for an upcoming horror film festival (Spook Screen 2018). I designed the logo and laurels for the festival and so made a short animation using my design for use in promotional material and to show before programmes during the festival.

Digital Matte Painting

I created this digital matte painting as part of a college assignment. I developed the concept and shot the footage in Blackrock Castle, Cork (moon and cliffs sourced online). This image was composited in Photoshop.

Wishes in the Wind, Short Film

This is a short film that I worked on during my second year on the Digital Animation Production course in LIT, Clonmel. I worked as part of a team of four to produce this project from conception to completion. My roles involved scriptwriting, production managing and background and character design (in collaboration). My primary role was that of compositor and in particular, creating the particle effects for the fairy character. I also animated the scene with the bride and the opening parallax shot. As well as this, I also worked on the sound design and music for the film.

3D Boat Composite

This was created as part of an exercise in practicing the implementation of CG elements with live action footage. The footage was tracked in PFTrack and the data was subsequently used in Maya to render out the 3d boat (sourced online) to match. Further alterations and compositing effects (colour grading and fog effects, etc) were added in After Effects.

Lip Sync Animation

A short lip sync animation of two characters conversing. I was asked to choose a short audio piece and develop a concept to further practice animation techniques, especially rigging and lip syncing in Toon Boom Harmony. I designed the characters, backgrounds and shots and composited the final piece in After Effects.

LEGO Ident Sequence

An experiment in stop motion using a Lego star wars ship. I created this as part of a hypothetical project for TRTE as an ident for younger viewers. I shot the spaceship against pieces of blue card and began removing LEGO pieces with each image. The footage was then composited in After Effects with some motion graphics added to suit.

Anticipation Exercise

Sneaking Exercise

Weight Exercise

A short exercise created with use of a pre-existing character in learning and practicing the idea of weight in animation.

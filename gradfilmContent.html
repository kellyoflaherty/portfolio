"The Light in the Darkness" is my forthcoming graduate short film which I am completing as part of my BSc in Digital Animation Production. The film will be created in 3D through Maya and Houdini.

The film tells the story of a young girl enveloped in darkness who suddenly finds a bulb of light floating before her, ushering her forward. In her determination to catch it, she must face a number of challenges.

As the project progresses, I will upload progress pictures and videos here!

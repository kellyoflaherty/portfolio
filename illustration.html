Spook Screen Logo

I created the logo for Deep Red Production's Spook Screen Film Festival happening in Cork in September 2018. The logo was created in Adobe Illustrator.

Spook Screen Laurels

As well as the logo, I also created the laurels for Deep Red Production's Spook Screen Film Festival (September 2018). These laurels would thus be used by those who have submitted films to the festival to promote their work. The laurels were created in Adobe Illustrator.

Gluten Freak (short film) Prop Labels

I created some graphic design labels to be placed on some props for a short film, "Gluten Freak" by Fergus Burke, filmed in June 2018. These labels were designed in Adobe Illustrator.

26 Poster 1

These posters were created for use in a fan-made video for Paramore's "26" (as yet unreleased) under the basic premise of a struggling actors life. I received green-screen imagery of the main actor and created these images to be placed in the background of the video. These were created in Adobe Photoshop.

Riverdale

A piece of fan art I created based upon Netflix's Riverdale. Created in Adobe Photoshop.

Wishes In The Wind Poster

A poster created to coincide with "Wishes in the Wind"; a short film that I worked on as part of my second year on the Digital Animation Production course in LIT, Clonmel. Created in Photoshop.

Wishes In The Wind Poster Mock-ups

Mock-ups produced whilst developing my design for the "Wishes in the Wind" poster.

Cowboy Character Design

Character design of a cowboy created as a college assignment. Once designed, the character was digitally painted to match a variation of weather situations. Created in Adobe Photoshop.

Concept Art: Death

A concept art piece created as an exercise in dynamic angles and digital painting. Created in Adobe Photoshop.

Even the Best Writer has to Erase

Illustration based on the above title as a prompt. Elements sourced online and composited to create this piece, in Photoshop.

Lady in Red Title

Title screen created for a visual novel flash game during my time studying Digital Media in Colaiste Stiofain Naofa. Created in Photoshop.

Zeus Character Design

Character design of baby Zeus. Created in Adobe Illustrator.

Hansel & Gretel Background Design

A hypothetical game background design based on the tale of Hansel and Gretel. Created in Adobe Illustrator.

Glucksman Poster

This poster was developed from photographs taken (by myself) of the Glucksman Gallery in University College, Cork. The brief was to focus on defined shapes to draw up a graphical poster for the gallery. Created in Photoshop.
